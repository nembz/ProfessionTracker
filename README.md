# ProfessionTracker

**ProfessionTracker** is a World of Warcraft addon designed to help players manage their professions and related currencies across multiple characters. It provides a centralized UI to track weekly goals, knowledge progression, and crafting resources.

## Features

### 1. Multi-Character Currency Tracking
Keep track of essential endgame crafting currencies across your entire account:
*   **Weekly Shards & Owned Shards:** Monitor your Shard of Dundun progress.
*   **Unalloyed Abundance:** Track your accumulated abundance for high-end crafting.
*   **Fused Vitality:** See how many vitality items you have in your bags.

### 2. Comprehensive Profession Oversight
A dedicated tab for professions provides a deep dive into your characters' expertise:
*   **Knowledge Points:** Tracks spent vs. unspent knowledge and total capacity for specialized trees.
*   **Skill Levels:** View current profession ranks and maximum caps.
*   **Moxie Tracking:** Monitor Artisan's Acuity/Mettle levels.
*   **Concentration:** Displays current Concentration levels with **live estimation** of recharge times until full.

### 3. Weekly Checklist & Progression
Never miss a weekly source of Knowledge Points:
*   **Weekly Treatises:** Tracks if you have used your profession-specific treatise for the week.
*   **Weekly Quests:** Status of profession-specific trainer and service quests.
*   **Gathering Progress:** Tracks the weekly limited drops for Herbalism, Mining, and Skinning.
*   **Darkmoon Faire:** Automatically detects if the Darkmoon Faire is active and tracks profession quest completion.
*   **One-time Treasures:** Tracks the discovery of hidden profession treasures in the world.

### 4. Equipment Tracking
*   **Epic Tools & Accessories:** Monitors if your characters have equipped their top-tier profession gear (Tools and Accessories).

### 5. Advanced User Interface
*   **Sortable Tables:** Click headers to sort by Name, Realm, Skill, Concentration, and more.
*   **Detailed Tooltips:** Hover over cells to see exactly which treasures are missing, equipment names, or precise concentration recharge intervals.
*   **Color-Coded Status:** Visual indicators (Green/Red/Gray) for completed, missing, or zero-value entries.
*   **Customizable Visibility:** Fully configurable settings to hide/show specific columns or characters to keep your view clean.
*   **Class-Colored Names:** Easy identification of character classes at a glance.

## Color Coded
* Everything is tracked with colors for each milestone reached

## Usage

*   **/pt**: Open the Profession Tracker main window.
*   **/pt reset**: Resets all stored character data and reloads the UI.

## Data Updates
The addon automatically updates character data when you:
*   Turn in quests.
*   Update currency.
*   Change equipment.
*   Loot items or close profession windows.
